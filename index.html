What is Girl Up Jabalpur ?

Girl Up Jabalpur is a club under the Girl Up campaign launched in 2010 by the UN Foundation. It is an initiative to help supports comprehensive programs that give girls in developing countries an equal chance for education, health, social and economic opportunities.
