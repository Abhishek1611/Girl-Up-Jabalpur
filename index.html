What is Girl Up Jabalpur ?

Girl Up Jabalpur is a club under the Girl Up campaign launched in 2010 by the UN Foundation. It is an initiative to help supports comprehensive programs that give girls in developing countries an equal chance for education, health, social and economic opportunities.

Breaking Barrier

Girl Up Jabalpur

Welcome to "Breaking Barriers" a podcast by Girl Up Jabalpur where we hope to learn from each others' experiences and broaden our horizons. By keeping open minds and hearts, we explore ways to break these barriers standing in our way. We hope to learn from each others' experiences while acknowledging that everyone's experiences are their own. We hope to get to know ourselves, as human beings, as social creatures, get to understand our surroundings and attempt to hold hands and be there for each other as we continuously experience, learn, fail and grow every day.
