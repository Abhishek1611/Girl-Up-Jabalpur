ABOUT Girl Up Jabalpur

Girl Up Jabalpur is a club founded under the Girl Up Campaign,
an initiative by the United Nations Foundation.

The Girl Up Campaign is all about helping girls and young women realise their true
potential and furthering the Gender Equality movement that has taken over the globe.

Hailing from Jabalpur and having seen the plight of both rural and urban women very closely,
we are deeply devoted to helping them. We became feminists at a very young age and want to
do our part in furthering the Feminist movement and clear the air around it. We want to help
create a world which is equal and is kind to one another.
Kindness is our passion, it's something we strive for in the world.
So this is us calling all the women and men out there, who support this cause,
to join us and help us do the best we can!
Thank you. All the love.
