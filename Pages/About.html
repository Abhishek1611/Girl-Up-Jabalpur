We Bridge Future for women and LGBTQIA+ community

Girl Up Jabalpur is a chapter of Girl Up - an initiative by the United Nations Foundation. Based in Jabalpur, a city in the state of Madhya Pradesh in India; it was incepted on 3rd January 2021 by our Founding Co-Presidents, Arni Khare (she/her) and Nandini Tandon (she/her). Since then, the team of 30+ visionary volunteers over the span of two years have successfully conducted 20+ events, both online and offline. A major sucess has been building a passionate community and voicing the need of the hour. This is only the beginning!
